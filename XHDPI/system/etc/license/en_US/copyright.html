Guideline to the Copyright Protection Complaints

Notification：
　　If the copyright owner of copyright (hereinafter as “Copyright owner”) considers Xiaomi Inc. (hereinafter as “Company”), in the download service, provide works infringe its Right of Communication of Information through Networks (hereinafter as “RCIN”) or be removed, or change its right to manage electronic information, the Copyright owner can submit written notice and require the Company delete this work or disconnect it. The notice should be signed by the Copyright owner, and also covered official seals if it is the entity.
　　Please note: if the Right Notice misstated, the one submit it will take all consequential legal liability (including but not limited to all compensation fees and counsel fees). If the preceding individual or entity is not sure if the materials the Company provides in service infringe its legal rights, the Company suggests consulting with professionals firstly. The notice should include content below:
　　1. The name, contact methods, address of the Copyright owner, and the copy of identification and entity registration paper;
　　2. The accurate name and network address required be removing or disconnecting because of the violation of copyright;
　　3. The preliminary materials evidencing infringing copyright, including but not limited to materials evidencing RCIN and the manuscripts;
　　The Copyright owner should take responsibilities for the authenticity of the notice. If not authentic, the Copyright owner will bear all legal liability. Once receiving the notice, the Company will immediately remove or disconnect suspected infringing works, and simultaneously forward the notice to the provider of the suspected works (hereinafter as “provider”).
Counter-notification:
　　If the provider considers the work provided not infringing others’ rights, you can write statements of the counter-notification and demand recovering the works or the connections. The counter-notification should be signed by the Copyright owner, and also covered official seals if it is the entity.
　　Counter-notification should include:
　　1. The name, contact methods, address of the provider, and the copy of identification and the entity registration paper;
　　2. The accurate name and network address required to be recovered or reconnected;
　　3. The preliminary materials evidencing not infringing copyright, including but not limited to materials evidencing RCIN and the manuscripts;　
　　The provider should take responsibilities for the authenticity of the counter-notification. If not authentic, the provider will bear all legal liability. Once receiving the counter-notification., the Company will immediately recover the works or its connections, and simultaneously forward the counter-notification to the Copyright owner. The Company will not take legal liability for recovery and the Copyright owner should not notify the Company to remover the works or disconnect any more.
